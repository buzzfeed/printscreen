“Everything is design.” — Paul Rand

PrintScreen is an NYC speaker series bringing designers together to hear from diverse and interesting people both inside and outside our expertise. We hope to highlight the power and pervasiveness of our craft, as well as provide a place for designers to come together, meet their peers and depart with a greater sense of purpose.

Seating will be limited by the laws of physics and our local fire codes, but we will both livestream and post recordings of each talk so everyone can enjoy them.
